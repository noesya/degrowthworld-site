We are an international grassroots collective. We can help you get started with degrowth:


  • how to form your own local degrowth collective

  • connect with fellow degrowthers

  • learn more about degrowth


Degrowth is the expansion of freedoms and wellbeing for all humans, within the limits of Earth, by reducing production and consumption to lighten ecological footprint, planned democratically in the spirit of social justice.
See here for an expanded definition: https://degrowth.org/definition/
We meet both in person and online to learn, discuss, and strategize ways to elevate the concept of degrowth into global public discourse.
